About Me

Kevin Ngo is a Restoration Techinician at Tallgrass Restoration LLC. He currently works to restore land to its native state through variuos methods like herbicide treatment or prescribed burns. In 2019, he was a part of the field museum native garden installion which had over 200,00 plants. He has some experience in HTML, CSS, MySQL, and ARCgis. He found an interest in computer science after taking a class in his second to last semester in college.

When he isn't on the computer he enjoys doing just about any sport like rock climbing, ultimate frisbee, soccer, etc. and he also like playing board games. He ran his first and probably only marathon in 2017. He enjoys watching cooking shows and failing to recreate some of the dishes.

Kevin has a Bachelors of Science in Natural Restoration and Environmental Sciences from the University of Illinois at Champaign-Urbana.
